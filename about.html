About SoulFitness

All About Our Gym

We are the largest fitness company in Canada and the fourth largest in the world. Proudly serving Canadians for more then 34 years, with over 279 clubs and growing.

At Soul Fitness our purpose is to give every Canadian the opportunity to live a fit and healthy life.

Soul Fitness has a roster of different classes to suit any fitness style, and they are included in your membership so you can try them all to find the right fit.

We ensure our clubs are as safe as possible for all our Members and Associates.

We are focused on collaborations in all areas of health,fitness and wellness.

Please make sure to connect with our staff for more inquiries on fitness classes and membership details.

Company's Dedication

Our Team at Soul Fitness is dedicated and focused on positively changing the lives of as many Canadians as possible.

The company aims to do this by becoming the largest corporately owned fitness club chani in the world.

The Soul Fitness standard has been put together by a dedicated team that is hard at work consulting with experts in public health, infection prevention, cleaning sanitization to ensure we are taking every step to fulfill our commitment to you during the unprecedented time of Covid-19.

We require our fitness members to have their face masks on at all times, and sanitize hands at all times. Thank You !!
